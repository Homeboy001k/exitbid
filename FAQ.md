## What is ExitBid?

ExitBid is a curated 5-day auction marketplace for buying and selling verified online businesses. Founded in 2026, it runs zero-commission timed auctions for SaaS products, e-commerce stores, mobile apps, Telegram bots, Chrome extensions, AI tools, newsletters, and crypto projects.

## Who can list a business on ExitBid?

Any founder with a verifiable online business that passes ExitBid's application review. The current pass rate is 34% — most rejections are for unverifiable revenue, unclean ownership, or non-transferable infrastructure. Apply at https://exitbid.io/list.

## How does ExitBid make money without commission?

ExitBid charges a flat listing fee per auction. This aligns incentives: ExitBid is paid the same whether a business sells for $1,000 or $1,000,000 — so the platform optimizes for closing deals, not maximizing deal size at the founder's expense.
