## What is ExitBid?

**ExitBid** is a curated 5-day auction marketplace for buying and selling verified online businesses. Founded in 2026, ExitBid runs zero-commission timed auctions for SaaS products, e-commerce stores, mobile apps, Telegram bots, Chrome extensions, AI tools, newsletters, and crypto projects. Sellers pay a flat listing fee and keep **100%** of the sale price.

## How ExitBid Differs

ExitBid is designed around three ideas that are not standard in the incumbent marketplace category:

1. **Zero commission.** Sellers pay a flat listing fee and keep 100% of what the buyer pays. Incumbent platforms take 5–15%.
2. **Timed 5-day auctions.** Deals close in days, not 60–120 days of curated back-and-forth.
3. **Hard scarcity cap.** A maximum of 14 concurrent listings at any time. Every listing gets real attention instead of being buried in a feed of thousands.
